# Creative Brand Names: Out of the Box, High Impact

## Strategy: Clean, Memorable, High Impact

Thinking beyond trading terminology to find names that are:
- Clean & simple (1-2 syllables)
- High impact (memorable, powerful)
- Unique (not generic trading terms)
- Professional (Bloomberg Terminal appropriate)
- Ownable (can own "the [name]" positioning)

---

## Evaluation Criteria

### Must-Haves:
- ✅ Clean (1-2 syllables, simple)
- ✅ High impact (memorable, powerful)
- ✅ Unique (not generic trading terms)
- ✅ Professional (Bloomberg Terminal appropriate)
- ✅ Ownable (can own "the [name]" positioning)

### Nice-to-Haves:
- ✅ Works for both platform and community
- ✅ Suggests speed, precision, or power
- ✅ Modern, sophisticated feel
- ✅ Short domain available
