# Blurb Analysis & Recommendations

## Current Blurb Assessment

### Strengths ✅
- Professional, factual tone
- No gimmicky language
- Clear capability description
- Strong credibility markers
- Covers key value props

### Areas for Improvement ⚠️

1. **Opening redundancy**: "Autonomous Trading Terminal" header + "autonomous trading terminal" in first sentence
2. **Vague language**: "runs strategies" - unclear what this adds beyond "executes trades"
3. **Value prop order**: Starts with natural language, but autonomy might be stronger differentiator
4. **Missing differentiation**: Doesn't clearly state what makes it unique vs competitors
5. **"price indicators"**: Too vague - could be more specific
6. **Flow**: Connection between paragraphs could be tighter
7. **Problem statement**: Doesn't clearly state the problem it solves

---

## Recommended Improvements

### Option 1: Autonomy-First (Stronger Differentiation)

**Autonomous Trading Terminal**

Deploy Terminal enables autonomous strategy deployment and execution across multiple exchanges. The platform accepts natural language strategy descriptions, compiles them into executable logic, and continuously monitors market conditions, executes trades, and manages positions without manual intervention.

The terminal integrates perpetual futures, prediction markets, and backtesting in a unified platform. Strategies respond automatically to funding rate changes, price movements, Polymarket events, and other market conditions.

Launch scheduled for Q1 2026 with support for Hyperliquid, Binance, Decibel, Lighter, Polymarket, and Kalshi.

**Backed by:** Aptos Foundation, GSR, Third Earth Capital. Partnered with Decibel (Aptos' perp DEX).

**Team experience:** Built stablecoins and perpetual futures protocols managing $250M+ in assets with $150M+ in trading volume. Additional experience includes chip-level hardware design at MediaTek and backend infrastructure at Zomato (50M+ MAUs). Team has shipped production-grade products securing $250M+ in assets since 2020.

**Changes:**
- Removed redundant "autonomous trading terminal" in first sentence
- Changed "runs strategies" to "manages positions" (clearer)
- Added "without manual intervention" (emphasizes autonomy)
- Changed "price indicators" to "price movements" (more specific)
- Tighter flow between paragraphs
