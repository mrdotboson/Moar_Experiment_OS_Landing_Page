# Product Blurb - Deploy Terminal

## Recommended Version

**Deploy Terminal | Deck | Twitter | TG**

**Autonomous Trading Terminal**

Deploy Terminal is an autonomous trading terminal that enables strategy deployment and execution across multiple exchanges. The platform accepts natural language strategy descriptions, compiles them into executable logic, and autonomously monitors market conditions, executes trades, and manages positions.

The terminal integrates perpetual futures, prediction markets, and backtesting capabilities in a unified platform. Strategies can be configured to respond to Polymarket events, funding rate changes, price movements, and other market conditions automatically.

Launch scheduled for Q1 2026 with support for Hyperliquid, Binance, Decibel, Lighter, Polymarket, and Kalshi.
