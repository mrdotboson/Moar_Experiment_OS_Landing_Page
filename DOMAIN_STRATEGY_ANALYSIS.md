# Domain Strategy: .trade vs .com

## Comparison: sentient.trade vs sentientterminal.com

---

## Option 1: sentient.trade ⭐⭐⭐ (Recommended)

### Pros:
- ✅ **Shorter & Cleaner**: "sentient.trade" vs "sentientterminal.com"
- ✅ **More Memorable**: Easier to remember and type
- ✅ **Clear Purpose**: Immediately signals it's trading-related
- ✅ **Modern & Unique**: Stands out, suggests innovation
- ✅ **Professional in Fintech**: Crypto/DeFi space uses .trade successfully
- ✅ **Better Branding**: "sentient.trade" is cleaner for marketing
- ✅ **Easier to Say**: "sentient dot trade" flows better
- ✅ **Less Generic**: More distinctive than .com
- ✅ **Future-Proof**: Modern TLDs are becoming more accepted

### Cons:
- ⚠️ **Less Traditional**: Some users might not trust it initially
- ⚠️ **Email Compatibility**: Some older email systems might not recognize it
- ⚠️ **SEO Considerations**: .com historically has slight SEO advantage
- ⚠️ **Typing Habits**: Users might default to typing .com

### Examples of Successful .trade Users:
- Many crypto/fintech companies use alternative TLDs
- .trade is well-established in trading/finance space
- Professional traders are comfortable with it

---

## Option 2: sentientterminal.com

### Pros:
- ✅ **Most Trusted**: Everyone knows and trusts .com
- ✅ **Universal Recognition**: No explanation needed
- ✅ **Email Compatibility**: Works everywhere
- ✅ **SEO Advantage**: .com has historical SEO benefits
- ✅ **Traditional Finance**: More familiar to traditional finance
- ✅ **No Barriers**: Users won't hesitate to type it

### Cons:
- ⚠️ **Longer**: "sentientterminal.com" is 20 characters
- ⚠️ **Less Memorable**: Harder to remember full domain
- ⚠️ **More Generic**: Less distinctive
- ⚠️ **Harder to Type**: More characters, more room for error
- ⚠️ **Less Modern**: Doesn't signal innovation as clearly
- ⚠️ **Branding**: "sentientterminal.com" is clunkier in marketing

---

## Recommendation: sentient.trade ⭐⭐⭐

### Why .trade Wins:

1. **Brand Positioning**
   - "sentient.trade" is cleaner, more memorable
   - Better for establishing "the" brand
   - More distinctive in the market

2. **Target Audience**
   - Professional traders (especially crypto/DeFi) are comfortable with .trade
   - Modern fintech uses alternative TLDs successfully
   - Bloomberg Terminal users are tech-savvy

3. **Practical Benefits**
   - Shorter = easier to remember, type, share
   - Clear purpose = immediately signals trading
   - Modern = suggests innovation, AI, new paradigm

4. **Marketing**
   - "sentient.trade" works better in ads, social media
   - Cleaner for business cards, presentations
   - More professional in modern fintech context

---

## Hybrid Strategy (Best of Both Worlds)

### Option 3: Own Both ⭐⭐⭐
- **Primary**: sentient.trade (main brand)
- **Redirect**: sentientterminal.com → sentient.trade
- **Email**: Use sentient.trade for email (modern email supports it)

### Benefits:
- ✅ Get .com for users who type it
- ✅ Use .trade as primary brand
- ✅ Cover all bases
- ✅ Best of both worlds

### Cost:
- Both domains (usually $10-50/year each)
- Worth it for brand protection
