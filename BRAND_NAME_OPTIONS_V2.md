# Brand Name Options V2: Trading Terminal Culture

## Strategy: Names That Work for Both Terminal & Traders

Following friend.com's approach but tailored for professional trading terminal culture. Names should:
- Appeal to Bloomberg Terminal users
- Work for both the platform AND the community
- Be unique, not generic
- Suggest precision, execution, edge
- Feel professional yet ownable

---

## Top Recommendations

- **Positioning**: "The edge" - your trading edge
- **Why it works**: 
  - Traders understand "edge" immediately

  - Short, powerful, professional
  - Ownable: "the edge" in trading
  - Suggests competitive advantage
- **Domain**: edge.trade, edge.com, getedge.com

  - "Your trading edge"

## Recommendation

**Go with EDGE** - it's the most resonant with traders, works perfectly for both the terminal and the community, and is highly ownable.

**Why EDGE wins**:
- Traders immediately understand "edge" = competitive advantage
- Works as: "Edge terminal" and "Edge traders"
- Short, powerful, professional
- Ownable: "the edge" in trading
- Not generic, trading-specific but not limiting

**Domain strategy**: 
- Primary: `edge.trade` (if available) or `getedge.com`
- Backup: `edge.com` (if budget allows)

**Brand positioning**:
- "The edge" - your trading edge
- "Edge terminal" - the platform
- "Edge traders" - the community
- Own the word "edge" in trading context

---

## Alternative: EXEC

If EDGE feels too competitive/aggressive, **EXEC** is the backup:
- More neutral, execution-focused
- Terminal-appropriate (like commands)
- Professional, Bloomberg-style
- Works for both platform and traders
