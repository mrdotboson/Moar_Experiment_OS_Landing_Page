# Brand Name Options for Autonomous Trading Platform

## Strategy: Own "The [Name]" Positioning

Following friend.com's approach - simple, ownable, memorable. The goal is to establish **"the"** brand for autonomous trading.

---

## Top Recommendations

### 1. **AUTO** ⭐ (Top Pick)
- **Positioning**: "The auto" - autonomous trading
- **Why it works**: 
  - Ultra-simple, 4 letters
  - Directly conveys automation
  - Easy to remember, type, say
  - Can own "auto" in trading context
  - Works as verb: "auto your trades"
- **Domain**: auto.trade, auto.com, getauto.com
- **Tagline options**: 
  - "The auto"
  - "Auto your trades"
  - "Autonomous trading, simplified"

### 2. **PILOT**
- **Positioning**: "The pilot" - your trading pilot
- **Why it works**:
  - Suggests guidance, autonomy, control
  - Professional, trustworthy feel
  - Works as verb: "pilot your portfolio"
- **Domain**: pilot.trade, pilot.com, getpilot.com
- **Tagline**: "Your trading pilot"

### 3. **ROBOT**
- **Positioning**: "The robot" - automated trading
- **Why it works**:
  - Direct, no explanation needed
  - Memorable, slightly playful but professional
  - Ownable in trading context
- **Domain**: robot.trade, robot.com, getrobot.com
- **Tagline**: "The robot for your trades"

### 4. **AUTO** (variant: **AUTO.TRADE**)
- **Positioning**: "The auto" 
- **Why it works**: Same as AUTO but with .trade domain for clarity
- **Domain**: auto.trade (perfect match)

## Evaluation Criteria

### Must-Haves:
- ✅ Simple (1-2 syllables, easy to spell)
- ✅ Memorable (sticks in mind)
- ✅ Ownable (can own "the [name]" positioning)
- ✅ Brandable (works as logo, domain)
- ✅ Conveys autonomy/automation (directly or indirectly)

### Nice-to-Haves:
- ✅ Works as verb ("[name] your trades")
- ✅ Professional feel (not too playful)
- ✅ Short domain available
- ✅ No negative connotations

---

## Top 3 Finalists

### 1. **AUTO** ⭐⭐⭐
**Best overall**: Simple, direct, ownable, works as verb

### 2. **PILOT** ⭐⭐
**Best professional feel**: Trustworthy, suggests guidance

### 3. **ROBOT** ⭐
**Most memorable**: Direct, no explanation needed

## Recommendation

**Go with AUTO** - it's the simplest, most direct, and most ownable. You can position as "The auto" for autonomous trading, and it works perfectly as both noun and verb.

**Domain strategy**: 
- Primary: `auto.trade` (if available) or `getauto.com`
- Backup: `auto.com` (if budget allows)

**Brand positioning**:
- "The auto" - autonomous trading
- "Auto your trades" - simple command
- Own the word "auto" in trading context
